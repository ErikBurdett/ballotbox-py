MapLibre. Counties and (when bundled) overlays: local GeoJSON under static/geo/. Legislative layers: U.S. Census TIGERweb Legislative; school districts: TIGERweb School; water districts: TCEQ. Not a substitute for official election maps. Re-run fetch_texas_legislative_geojson if bundled files predate changes. Basemap: MapLibre demo tiles.
